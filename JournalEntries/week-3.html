Week 3

September 22 - 26, 2025

This week we worked with APIs, specifically the weather API and the World Air Quality Index API.

Reflections

Understanding the basic concept was fine - APIs let you grab data from other sources. The in-class exercise started simple but got confusing pretty quickly, mainly due to the instructions being very compact, I accidently skipped a few steps and had to go back after realizing.

Working with my teammate helped a lot. When one of us got stuck, the other could usually spot the problem, in my case it was realizing that I skipped a couple lines while trying to complete the exercise. We managed to finish it in the end, which felt good.

It's interesting that you can pull real-time air quality data from anywhere in the world just by making a request. Makes you think about how much data is floating around out there, waiting to be used in creative ways.

I'm curious about using this kind of data for art projects instead of just displaying numbers on a screen.
